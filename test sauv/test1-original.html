Biography

04 January - Capricorn

The world is in the hands of those who dare to dream and take the chanche to live their dreams.

Paulo Coelho
1981 / 1984

Anna Safroncik was born in Kiev in 1981. Both parents are stars of the National Theatre of Opera and Ballet of Kiev and Anna spent her childhood literally behind the scenes. From the age of 5 she began her journey in the world of entertainment by being casted in numerous plays in the theatre.

When she was 7 years old she attended the National Academy of Performing Arts in Kiev, where she began to study music, piano, singing, dancing and acting.

   
1984 / 2008

While already living in Italy, in 1998, she was elected Miss Toscana within the Miss Italia contest. Since then she moved to Rome and debuted on the big screen.

She was casted as the female lead alongside two of the most important both Actor and Italian director. Soon arrived important roles in some of the most successful tv-series .

Meanwhile she starred as Cleopatra in the theatrical musical with the same name and was then selected as the face of many of the advertisement campaigns for some major fashion labels.

In 2004 Anna started one of the most popular Italian TV series, "Cento Vetrine", followed by an average of 5 million viewers. Her role conquered the affection of the female audience.

2008 / 2011

In 2008 she acted in the costume tv series in the role of the Dark Lady.

Since 2008 Anna worked on several projects, every year challenging herself with different roles and projects. She performed as Dark Lady, Love Heroine, Action Thriller all star roles which gave her great popularity and large public affection throughout Italy. Still in 2009 she starred in "Ritmo della vita" where Anna demonstrated her versatility playing the role of a professional dancer showing her excellent preparation in dance.

In 2012 Anna was the first name of "Le Tre Rose di Eva" who registered the biggest success for Italian Channel 5 of the last 10 years, becoming one of the most popular shows on TV and thus bringing Anna an extraordinary success, awards and the audience's hearts. The greatly awaited third season will be soon on screen in 2015. Today Anna is one of the most wanted and best paid actresses in the Italian market.
